DIY Where's Waldo Scavenger Hunt

Turn your home or backyard into a live-action Waldo game! You can easily set up a customized Waldo search-and-find game using our character printables:

  1. Print and Cut: Download the 10-page character pack card above and print it out. Cut out all character figures and hidden items.
  2. Hide the Pieces: Hide the paper standees around your house, bedrooms, or backyard when the players are not looking.
  3. Begin the Search: Give players a list of characters they need to find (e.g. Waldo, Wenda, Woof's tail, Wizard's scroll).
  4. Compete and Win: The searcher who returns with the most items wins! Alternatively, use a timer to see if kids can beat their personal records.
